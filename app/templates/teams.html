About Formula 1 Teams

Formula 1 teams are the backbone of the sport, each bringing their unique engineering expertise and racing heritage to the grid. Each team designs and builds their own cars within the regulations set by the FIA, competing for both the Drivers' and Constructors' Championships.

Team Structure

F1 teams employ hundreds of staff across various departments including aerodynamics, mechanics, strategy, and operations.

Budget Cap

Since 2021, F1 teams operate under a cost cap to level the playing field and make the sport more sustainable.

Development

Teams continuously develop their cars throughout the season, bringing upgrades to improve performance.
